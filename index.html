Transformative Power of Yoga
Open Your Heart

I strive to enlighten my students to the transformative power of yoga by broadening their practice, opening their hearts, and introducing them to the idea of personal and interpersonal communication through yoga. I seek to illuminate each student's practice through humor, kindness, and my passion for creating connections.

Choose Your Own Adventure
Something for everyone

My goal as an instructor is to allow people to forge their own path when it comes to Yoga. I am here to help you acheive your goals, and I offer a wide range of classes and times to get you there.

Body and Mind Connection
Opening a Dialogue

Yoga is about opening a dialogue between your body and your mind. Bring your own creative spirit and together we will create the practice that is best for you!
