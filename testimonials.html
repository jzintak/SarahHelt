I believe her breathing techniques and call for calmnest to be the greatest asset I have now in dealing with these issues. I cannot thank Sarah enough for helping me with these issues, she has been a godsend to me and I have never looked backed. She will be deeply missed by me and I couldn't think of where I would be without her. Thank you Sarah! "
