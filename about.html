About Sarah

A performer at heart Sarah Elizabeth Helt began singing and dancing at an early age. Fascinated by movement and the story it can create, Sarah stumbled into her first Ashtanga yoga class in New York City Autumn 2005. After a few years, and a big move to Iowa City, Iowa she practiced many different styles sporadically through her early twenties. Until one particular college professor inspired her to join his semester-long course in Baptiste-based Power Vinyasa. "My teacher often encouraged me to consider teaching, but I would brush him off. Little did I know how much joy it would bring into my life."

After college, the Southern Hemisphere lured her in and a year of true soul searching began. Sarah called Melbourne, Australia home for a short stint where the sweet smell of sea salt drew her to Bali, Indonesia. It was here with Santosha School of Yoga that Sarah completed her 200 hour yoga teacher training.

Now a full-time yoga instructor in Chicago, IL. Sarah teaches with enthusiasm and delight. Striving for a Vinyasa class that challenges every individual in their unique way and a Yin class that takes you deeply into the recesses of your body-mind to find the through line of who you are.

In 2014 Sarah continued her yogic studies in Vancouver, B.C. Canada with Bernie Clark. Where she completed 50 hours of continuing education in Yin Yoga. Which, through the help of her inspiring students, she has adapted for the handicapped.

Sarah teaches at numerous studios through-out Chicago. She is also available for corporate, private, and small group classes. For all questions and inquiries please e-mail: SarahElizabethHelt@gmail.com
